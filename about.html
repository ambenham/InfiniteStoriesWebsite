We believe minorities’ voices deserve to be heard.

For centuries, people who belong to a minority group have been sidelined — especially those who belong to several minorities.

No more.

We believe all stories deserve to be read. To balance the scales, our team created Infinite Stories, a bookstore that only sells books about minorities, and books written by minorities.

It’s time to give the silenced voices an audience. Join our movement, and explore our books here.
